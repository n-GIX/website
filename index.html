Who Are We?

nGIX is a community-owned group dedicated to web hacking and cheat development. There is no single individual behind nGIX — it’s a collective effort of many contributors.

We are a growing community of early-stage developers and hackers seeking to join broader hacking communities.

Our goal is to create a platform for sharing knowledge, tools, and resources related to web hacking and cheat development.
